Pi Tracker Partnership Eligibility Terms

Eligibility Requirements

Pi Tracker is open to partnering with Pi Network-based projects and Discord communities that contribute positively to the ecosystem. To ensure quality and compliance, all potential partners must meet the following eligibility criteria:

1. General Requirements

The project or community must be solely focused on Pi Network. General crypto projects that merely feature Pi Network but are not built around it will not be eligible at this time.

All partnerships must comply with Pi Network’s terms and guidelines, as well as all applicable local laws and regulations.

Applications will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and some terms may be negotiated if the project demonstrates strong potential and commitment.

2. Project Eligibility

The project must be legitimate and active, with a clear purpose and ongoing development.

It must not engage in scams, misleading activities, or unauthorized financial services.

A publicly accessible website, social media presence, or other verifiable information is preferred but not mandatory, as long as the project can demonstrate credibility.

The project must not directly compete with Pi Tracker but should complement or enhance its offerings.

Some degree of transparency is expected, but full disclosure of internal operations is not required.

3. Discord Community Eligibility

To partner with Pi Tracker, Discord servers must have at least 500 members and be semi-active, meaning they have regular engagement.

The Pi Tracker bot must be added to the server for integration purposes.

Servers must follow Discord’s terms of service and maintain a safe and respectful community.

4. Partnership Benefits

Approved partners will be featured on the Pi Tracker bot’s profile, official website, and the /affiliates command.

Partners may receive additional promotional opportunities and cross-collaboration benefits.

5. Termination of Partnership

Pi Tracker reserves the right to terminate a partnership if the project or community becomes inactive, violates terms, or engages in behavior that could harm the Pi Network ecosystem.

6. Contact

For partnership inquiries, please reach out via the official contact page.
