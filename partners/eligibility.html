Pi Tracker Partnership Eligibility Terms

Eligibility Requirements

Pi Tracker is open to partnering with Pi Network-based projects and Discord communities that contribute positively to the ecosystem. To ensure quality and compliance, all potential partners must meet the following eligibility criteria:

1. General Requirements

  • The project or community must be solely focused on Pi Network. General crypto projects that merely feature Pi Network but are not built around it will not be eligible at this time.
  • All partnerships must comply with Pi Network’s terms and guidelines, as well as all applicable local laws and regulations.
  • Applications will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis, and some terms may be negotiated if the project demonstrates strong potential and commitment.

2. Project Eligibility

  • The project must be legitimate and active, with a clear purpose and ongoing development.
  • It must not engage in scams, misleading activities, or unauthorized financial services.
  • A publicly accessible website, social media presence, or other verifiable information is preferred but not mandatory, as long as the project can demonstrate credibility.
  • The project must not directly compete with Pi Tracker but should complement or enhance its offerings.
  • Some level of transparency is expected, but full public disclosure of the team or roadmap is not strictly required if the project can prove legitimacy through other means.
  • The project must be willing to acknowledge Pi Tracker as a partner on its platform.

3. Discord Server Eligibility

  • The server must be solely focused on Pi Network and support its ecosystem in a meaningful way.
  • The server must have a minimum of 500 members and be at least semi-active (regular discussions, engagement, and activity).
  • The Pi Tracker bot must be in the server to verify engagement and provide access to its features.
  • The server must follow ethical community management practices, with no involvement in scams, misinformation, or disruptive behavior.
  • A partnership acknowledgment (such as a role, announcement, or channel mention) is required.

4. Compliance & Ethical Standards

  • All partnerships must strictly adhere to Pi Network’s terms and policies.
  • Projects must not engage in Pi-to-fiat transactions if they violate Pi Network’s guidelines.
  • Partners must not make misleading claims about Pi’s utility, exchangeability, or their project’s roadmap.

5. Partnership Benefits

  • Approved partners will be featured on the Pi Tracker bot’s profile.
  • Approved partners will be listed on the Pi Tracker website.
  • Approved partners will be displayed in the `/affiliates` command within the bot.
  • Approved partners will be eligible for cross-promotion in Pi Tracker’s community spaces (where applicable).
