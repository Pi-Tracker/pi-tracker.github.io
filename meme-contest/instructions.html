Pi Network Meme Contest - Instructions

How to Participate

Submit your meme via Google Forms.

Ensure your meme is Pi-related and clearly visible.

Memes must be original. Generic crypto memes will not count.

Be sure to include your Discord @username or Reddit u/handle in the submission form.

Voting will take place via Google Forms and will be determined by community votes.

Any updates will be posted at https://pi-tracker.xyz/meme-contest/, the r/PiNetwork subreddit, and Pi Network Community Discord.

Have Questions?

Reach out via Discord or Reddit. Thank you for participating!
