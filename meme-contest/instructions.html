Pi Network Meme Contest - Instructions

Instructions for Participation

To participate in the Pi Network Meme Contest, follow these guidelines:

1. Submit your meme via the Google Forms link, ensuring you include a high-quality image or video of your meme.

2. Send your Pi contribution to the specified Pi Network wallet address to help increase the prize pool. Be sure to include your Discord @username, Pi @username, or Reddit u/handle in the memo so your contribution can be properly credited and accounted for.

3. Voting will take place in the r/PiNetwork subreddit, and winners will be determined by the community vote. The prize pool will be split accordingly if it reaches 50 Pi or more.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via Discord or Reddit. Thank you for participating!
