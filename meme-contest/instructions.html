Pi Network Meme Contest - Instructions

Instructions for Participation

To participate in the Pi Network Meme Contest, follow these guidelines:

Submit your meme via the Google Forms link, ensuring you include a high-quality image or video of your meme.

Your meme should be original. Any generic crypto memes will not be counted for. Be sure to include your Discord @username or Reddit u/handle when submitting so your meme can be properly credited and accounted for.

Voting will take place via Google Forms, and winners will be determined by community vote.

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via Discord or Reddit. Thank you for participating!
