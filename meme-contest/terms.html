Pi Network Meme Contest Terms & Conditions

By submitting your meme to the Pi Network Meme Contest, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. No Mandatory Entry Fee

Participation in this contest is completely free. There is no entry fee required to enter and participate.

2. Optional Contributions to the Prize Pool

Contributions to the prize pool are optional. If you wish to contribute, you may do so by sending Pi to the specified Pi Network wallet. These contributions will go directly to the prize pool.

3. Prize Pool Split

The prize pool will only be split among the top three winners if the total prize pool (including the initial prize) reaches at least 50 Pi. If the total prize pool is below 50 Pi, the prize will be adjusted accordingly.

If the prize pool reaches or exceeds 50 Pi, the top three winners will share the prize pool as follows:

  • 1st Place: 50% of the prize pool
  • 2nd Place: 30% of the prize pool
  • 3rd Place: 20% of the prize pool

4. Eligibility

  • You must be a member of the Pi Network to participate.
  • You must be 18 years or older to enter this contest.
  • This contest is open to all Pi Network users, but it is not sponsored or affiliated with Pi Network.

5. Submission Rules

  • Only one meme can be submitted per participant.
  • By submitting a meme, you grant the organizer (Pi Tracker) permission to use your meme for promotional purposes related to this contest.

6. Voting

  • Voting will take place on the r/PiNetwork subreddit or via Google Forms (to be decided), and the winners will be determined based on community votes and/or judges' scores.
  • Voting is final and non-reversible.

7. Legal Disclaimer

  • This contest is not a lottery or gambling.
  • The organizer is not responsible for any legal consequences related to participation in this contest or any disputes that may arise.
  • Participants are responsible for ensuring that their participation complies with local laws and regulations regarding contests and giveaways.

8. Pi Network Disclaimer

This contest is independent and not affiliated with or sponsored by Pi Network. Pi Network is a decentralized network, and Pi is not yet recognized as an official currency.

9. Changes or Cancellations

The organizer reserves the right to modify, suspend, or cancel the contest at any time for any reason.
