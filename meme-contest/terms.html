Pi Network Meme Contest Terms & Conditions

1. No Mandatory Entry Fee

Participation in this contest is completely free. There is no entry fee required to enter and participate.

2. Optional Contributions to the Prize Pool

Contributions to the prize pool are optional. If you wish to contribute, you may do so by sending Pi to the specified Pi Network wallet. These contributions will go directly to the prize pool.

3. Prize Pool Split

The prize pool will only be split among the top three winners if the total prize pool (including the initial prize) reaches at least 50 Pi. If the prize pool is below 50 Pi, the first-place winner will take the entire prize pool.

Example: If the prize pool reaches 50 Pi:

  • 1st Place: 25 Pi (50% of the prize pool)
  • 2nd Place: 15 Pi (30% of the prize pool)
  • 3rd Place: 10 Pi (20% of the prize pool)

4. Eligibility

  • You must be a member of the Pi Network to participate.

  • This contest is open to all Pi Network users, but it is not sponsored or affiliated with Pi Network.

5. Submission Rules

6. Voting

  • Voting will take place on the r/PiNetwork subreddit or via Google Forms (to be decided), and the winners will be determined based on community votes and/or judges' scores.
  • Voting is final and non-reversible.
